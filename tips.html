Dental Tips and Fun Facts

Essential Dental Care Tips

  • 1.Brush your teeth twice a day with fluoride toothpaste
  • 2.Floss daily to remove plaque between teeth
  • 3.Limit sugary foods and drinks to prevent cavities
  • 4.Replace your toothbrush every 3-4 months
  • 5.Visit your dentist regularly for check-ups and cleanings

Fun Dental Facts

  • The enamel(white part of your tooth) is the hardest susbstance in your body.
  • No two people have the same set of teeth, they are as unique as fingerprints.
  • The average person spends 45-70 seconds brushing daily, thought 2-3 minutes is recommended
